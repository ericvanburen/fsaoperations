PCA Rep

The basic collection agency role includes, a similar role to that of the CSR, including access to view all borrower demographic, historical, payment detail and loan (debt) detail information. This role has the capability to update demographic data, establish billing plans, enter notes, update statuses and tags and access work lists and work groups. Action/Result codes are to be updated, as necessary, given the steps required to resolve a phone call, dispute, complaint or request a compromise payoff.

PCA Manager

This role includes all functionality of the PCA Rep plus it can update work lists and work groups, as required to adjust workloads and add or remove Reps under their control to various work lists or work groups. Additionally, this role can approve compromises and complete litigation packages and account returns moving the status appropriately to request FSA review and create/modify reports for viewing or export.

PCA IT

The IT role includes all view and update capabilities of the PCA Rep and Manager, including access to view/update borrower data and establish billing plans, create/modify reports, plus the ability to receive/transmit files.
